## Design Notes

- **Tone:** Professional, direct, transparent. Avoid hype.
- **Length:** Under 200 words of actual copy (excludes section headers).
- **Evidence:** References to LAB-003, LAB-004, LAB-005 with concrete metrics.
- **CTA Clarity:** Single primary action (join waitlist), with secondary option to wait for public results.
- **Honesty about limitations:** Limited to 50 participants, clear about what's still being built.

## Channel Fit

- **Email:** Primary distribution channel. Personalizable, allows for follow-up sequences.
- **Social Media (LinkedIn, Twitter):** Short version with link to landing page.
- **Direct Outreach:** Technical communities (Discord, Slack groups) where agent researchers gather.

## Timing

- Send 2 weeks before beta launch (aligns with 24 Mar 2026 target)
- Follow-up 3 days after initial send (for non-responders)
- Final reminder 24 hours before beta opens

## Metrics

- **Open Rate:** Target 40%+ (subject line testing)
- **Click Rate:** Target 15%+ (CTA clarity and landing page relevance)
- **Waitlist Signups:** Target 25-30% of email recipients (conversion goal)
