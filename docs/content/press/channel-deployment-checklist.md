## Press/Media Channel

### Assets Required
- [ ] Press release (1.5-2K words)
- [ ] Key messaging points
- [ ] Founder headshot (high-res)
- [ ] Product screenshots (3-5)
- [ ] Logo pack (primary + inverse)
- [ ] Media contact email

### Deployment Steps
1. **T-5 days:** Finalize press list (tech press, AI blogs)
2. **T-3 days:** Send embargoed release to tier-1 contacts
3. **T-2 days:** Upload assets to press kit page
4. **T-1 day:** Confirm embargo agreements
5. **T-0 6AM:** Press release goes live on website
6. **T-0 9AM:** Follow up with tier-2 contacts
7. **T+1 day:** Monitor coverage, respond to inquiries

### Success Metrics
| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Tier-1 Coverage | 2+ articles |
| Tier-2 Coverage | 5+ articles |
| Syndication | 10+ pickups |
| Media Inquiries | 5+ contacts |
