## LinkedIn Channel Fit

### Audience Profile

| Attribute | Primary | Secondary |
|-----------|---------|-----------|
| **Role** | AI/ML Engineer | Technical Founder |
| **Company Size** | Startup (10-50) | Scale-up (50-200) |
| **Technical Level** | Senior | Staff/Principal |
| **Interest** | Agent systems, memory, RAG | AI infrastructure, MLOps |
| **Buying Power** | Technical recommender | Decision maker |
| **Location** | US (EST/PST), EU (London/Berlin) | — |

### Best Times to Post

| Day | Time (EST) | Reasoning |
|-----|------------|-----------|
| Tuesday | 9:00-10:00 AM | Peak workday engagement |
| Wednesday | 9:00-10:00 AM | Mid-week focus |
| Thursday | 8:00-9:00 AM | Pre-meeting scroll |
| **Avoid** | Friday PM, Weekend | Low engagement |

### Engagement Metrics to Track

| Metric | Definition | Target | Tool |
|--------|------------|--------|------|
| Impressions | Post views | 5,000+ | LinkedIn Analytics |
| Engagement Rate | (Reactions + Comments) / Impressions | 3%+ | LinkedIn Analytics |
| Click-Through Rate | Clicks / Impressions | 2%+ | LinkedIn Analytics |
| Comment Quality | Technical questions, not spam | 50%+ quality | Manual review |
| Follower Growth | New followers from post | 100+ | LinkedIn Analytics |

### Audience Signals

**Positive:**
- Comments mentioning "agents", "memory", "RAG"
- Profile views from AI/ML roles
- Connection requests from target companies
- Shares to technical groups

**Negative:**
- Generic "interesting" comments
- Views without engagement
- Shares to non-technical audiences

## Twitter/X Channel Fit

### Audience Profile

| Attribute | Primary | Secondary |
|-----------|---------|-----------|
| **Role** | Developer | AI Researcher |
| **Technical Level** | Mid-Senior | Academic |
| **Interest** | Open source, tools, benchmarks | Papers, experiments |
| **Engagement Style** | Replies, quote tweets | Retweets, bookmarks |
| **Influencers** | @karpathy, @soumith, @ylecun followers | AI Twitter regulars |
| **Location** | Global (24/7), US/EU peak | — |

### Best Times to Post

| Day | Time (EST) | Reasoning |
|-----|------------|-----------|
| Tuesday | 9:00-11:00 AM | Dev morning routine |
| Wednesday | 9:00-11:00 AM | Mid-week scroll |
| Thursday | 10:00 AM-12:00 PM | Pre-lunch engagement |
| **Avoid** | Monday AM, Friday PM | Meeting-heavy, wind-down |

### Engagement Metrics to Track

| Metric | Definition | Target | Tool |
|--------|------------|--------|------|
| Impressions | Tweet views | 10,000+ | Twitter Analytics |
| Engagement Rate | (Likes + RTs + Replies) / Impressions | 4%+ | Twitter Analytics |
| Link Clicks | CTA link clicks | 200+ | Twitter Analytics |
| Thread Completion | Users reading all tweets | 60%+ | Manual estimate |
| Reply Sentiment | Positive/neutral vs negative | 80%+ positive | Manual review |

### Audience Signals

**Positive:**
- Replies with technical questions
- Quote tweets with commentary
- Retweets from AI influencers
- Bookmark saves (intent signal)

**Negative:**
- Ratio attempts (negative replies)
- Spam/bot replies
- Low thread completion
- Immediate unfollows

## Cross-Channel Comparison

| Factor | LinkedIn | Twitter/X |
|--------|----------|-----------|
| **Reach Potential** | Medium (network-based) | High (viral possible) |
| **Audience Quality** | Higher intent | Broader mix |
| **Engagement Depth** | Longer comments | Quick reactions |
| **Conversion Path** | Direct to form | Awareness → LinkedIn |
| **Content Lifespan** | 24-48 hours | 2-6 hours |
| **Best for** | Qualified leads | Awareness |
