## Risk Assessment

### Risk 1: Low Engagement
**Probability:** Medium (new account, no existing community reputation)
**Impact:** <10 impressions, <5 engagements/week
**Mitigation:**
- Focus on high-value replies over posting volume
- Engage with established community members first
- Provide genuinely helpful technical answers
- Be patient building reputation (2-4 weeks for credibility)

### Risk 2: Spam Reports
**Probability:** Low (following community guidelines, value-first approach)
**Impact:** Account suspension, reputation damage
**Mitigation:**
- Strict adherence to community guidelines
- No self-promotion without value
- Vary content types (threads, replies, retweets)
- Monitor engagement signals (downvotes, blocks)

### Risk 3: Time Allocation Conflict
**Probability:** Medium (if Week 2 fully unblocked, need to manage 4 channels)
**Impact:** Reduced Twitter cadence, lower engagement quality
**Mitigation:**
- Prioritize channels by performance (data-driven allocation)
- Use content syndication (one piece repurposed across channels)
- Batch engagement activities (morning: monitor, afternoon: engage)

### Risk 4: Technical Depth Too High
**Probability:** Low (Faintech Lab has strong technical foundation)
**Impact:** Community doesn't understand content, low engagement
**Mitigation:**
- Use analogies and visualizations
- Explain jargon in simple terms
- Provide code examples and demos
- Ask questions to verify understanding
