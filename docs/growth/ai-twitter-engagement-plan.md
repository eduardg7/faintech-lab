## Risk Mitigation

### Avoid Spammy Self-Promotion
- **Rule:** 7:1 value-to-promotion ratio
- **Definition:** 7 helpful/informative posts for every 1 pitch post
- **Penalty:** Pause 24h if community signals fatigue

### Timing Strategy
- **Peak hours:** 09:00-12:00 EET and 18:00-21:00 EET
- **Avoid:** 03:00-06:00 EET (low engagement)
- **Cadence:** 2-3 hours between posts (not burst posting)

### Community Signals to Monitor
- **Negative signals:** "This is spam", "Another AI tool ad", low engagement-to-view ratio
- **Positive signals:** Technical questions about implementation, requests for paper, follow-up research interests
- **Action on negative:** Pause, pivot content strategy, apologize if over-promotional
