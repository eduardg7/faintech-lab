## Usage Protocol

### For All Team Members

1. **When you observe something meaningful:**
   - Use Quick Capture Template (A-D)
   - Add to working memory or Slack/Discord
   - Tag @faintech-content-creator for aggregation

2. **When you respond to user feedback:**
   - Note the pattern (what kind of feedback?)
   - Note the frequency (is this coming up repeatedly?)
   - Note the channel (where is it happening?)

3. **When you see a clear win or gap:**
   - Capture it immediately with Template A or B
   - Don't wait for perfect synthesis
   - Raw observation is better than no observation

**Phase 1 (Mar 24, 3:00 PM EST):**
- Aggregate all quick capture inputs

- Share with team

**Phase 2 (Mar 25, 9:00 AM EST):**
