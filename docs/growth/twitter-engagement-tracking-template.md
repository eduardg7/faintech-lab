## Action Triggers

### If Engagement Rate < 2% for 2+ consecutive tweets:
- **Action:** Pivot content strategy (too technical/too promotional)
- **Analysis:** Review top-performing AI community tweets for patterns

### If Zero Technical Conversations After 3+ Tweets:
- **Action:** Increase question-based content ("What's your biggest challenge with...?")
- **Analysis:** Technical questions drive engagement in AI communities

### If Link Clicks > 50 but Signups = 0:
- **Action:** Investigate landing page conversion friction
- **Handoff:** faintech-product-designer (UX optimization, Week 2 GTM coordination)

### If Tweet Gets 1,000+ Impressions with Low Engagement (<1%):
- **Action:** Viral reach but poor targeting - refine hashtag/mention strategy
- **Analysis:** High impressions = visibility; low engagement = wrong audience
