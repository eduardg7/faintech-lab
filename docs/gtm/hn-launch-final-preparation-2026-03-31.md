### Decision 2: How Aggressive Should Upvote Request Be?

**Options:**
1. **No upvote request** (conservative)
   - Pros: Zero risk of HN ring detection
   - Cons: Relies entirely on organic early velocity
   - Success probability: 30-40%

2. **Soft upvote request** (recommended)
   - Pros: Boosts early velocity within acceptable risk
   - Cons: Small risk of ring detection if too aggressive
   - Success probability: 50-60%
   - Implementation: "If you find this interesting, please consider upvoting. Thanks!"

3. **Aggressive upvote request** (not recommended)
   - Pros: Maximizes early velocity
   - Cons: High risk of HN ring detection (IP sharing, voting circles)
   - Success probability: 70-80% (if not flagged)

**Recommendation:** Soft upvote request. Include gentle language in launch notification: "We're launching on HN tomorrow at 17:00 EET. If you find the post interesting, please consider upvoting. No pressure - we value genuine engagement."
