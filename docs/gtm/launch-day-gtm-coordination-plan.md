## Contingency Scenarios

### Scenario A: Low Engagement (<50 expected signups by Day 1)
- **Trigger:** <50 signups by 18:00 on launch day
- **Response:**
  - Amplify through 2nd-tier network (30% of contacts)
  - Post 2 additional value pieces (experiment insights, R&D notes)
  - Direct outreach to 5 high-value beta candidates
- **Escalation (if <30 by Day 3):** CMO → CEO decision on pivot strategy

### Scenario B: Medium Engagement (50-100 expected signups by Day 1)
- **Trigger:** 50-100 signups by 18:00 on launch day
- **Response:** Continue planned Day 1-3 rhythm, monitor for acceleration

### Scenario C: High Engagement (>100 expected signups by Day 1)
- **Trigger:** >100 signups by 18:00 on launch day
- **Response:**
  - CSM onboarding capacity check (scale up if needed)
  - Prepare surge capacity documentation
  - Capture testimonials from early users (with permission)
  - Consider accelerating to full launch if beta feedback is strong

### Scenario D: Technical Failure (access codes broken, signup flow fails)
- **Trigger:** Any critical signup/access failure
- **Response:**
  - IMMEDIATE (soft deadline: 15min): Alert cto + cmo
  - POST-MORTEM (48h): Document incident, root cause, fix
  - RESCHEDULE (48h buffer): Announce corrected launch date
- **Escalation:** CMO → CEO within 1hr if not resolved
