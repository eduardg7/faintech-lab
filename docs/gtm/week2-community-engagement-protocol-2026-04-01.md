## Purpose

Define clear engagement protocol for Reddit and HN launch to maximize community interaction, minimize negative sentiment, and track performance metrics for Week 2 GTM.

---

## Phase 1: Before Posting (Pre-Launch)

### Reddit (5 posts prepared)

**Timeline:** 2-4 hours before each post (April 4, 6, 8)

**Actions:**
1. **Review subreddit rules** (sidebar, posting guidelines)
   - Check if technical posts are allowed
   - Verify self-promotion policies
   - Review recent similar posts for sentiment

2. **Engage with community** (30-45 minutes)
   - Comment on 5-10 recent, relevant posts in target subreddits
   - Provide value, no links, establish presence
   - Build karma before self-promotion

3. **Optimize post timing**
   - Target window: Tue-Thu 9-11 AM EET
   - Monitor subreddit activity patterns
   - Adjust based on timezone of target audience

4. **Pre-post checklist**
   - Verify all links work (demo URL, UTM tracking)
   - Proofread for tone (helpful, not salesy)
   - Prepare response templates for common questions

### HN Launch (Today, April 1, 17:00 EET)

**Timeline:** Before 17:00 EET

**Actions:**
1. **Support CMO launch execution**
   - Monitor HN account warm-up status
   - Prepare upvote/downvote response plan
   - Track launch post for early engagement

2. **Engagement readiness**
   - Be available to respond to comments within 5-10 minutes
   - Prepare technical details for product questions
   - Document "Show HN" best practices

## Phase 2: During Posting (Live Engagement)

### Reddit Posts

**Timeline:** First 2 hours after posting, then daily for 3 days

**Actions:**
1. **Immediate response** (first 30 minutes)
   - Respond to every comment (helpful, informative)
   - Clarify technical points without being defensive
   - Provide additional value (resources, insights)

2. **Engagement monitoring** (ongoing)
   - Track upvotes, comments, sentiment
   - Identify potential advocates or critics
   - Avoid getting into arguments (be helpful, not right)

3. **Content amplification** (if positive engagement)
   - Share insights in related threads (with context)
   - Cross-reference similar discussions
   - Build on positive momentum

### HN Launch

**Timeline:** April 1, 17:00 EET - April 3, 00:00 EET

**Actions:**
1. **Early engagement** (first 2 hours critical)
   - Respond to every technical question
   - Provide transparent, helpful answers
   - Avoid hype, focus on substance

2. **Negative sentiment handling**
   - Don't get defensive (HN hates hype)
   - Acknowledge valid criticism
   - Offer concrete solutions or improvements

3. **Performance tracking**
   - Monitor upvote/downvote ratio
   - Track comments for sentiment trends
   - Document key insights for iteration

## Phase 3: After Posting (Analysis & Iteration)

### Daily Tracking (3 days post-launch)

**Metrics to capture:**
1. **Engagement metrics**
   - Reddit: upvotes, comments, karma changes
   - HN: upvotes, comments, ranking

2. **Conversion metrics** (from tracking template)
   - UTM clicks per channel
   - Signup conversions
   - Time to first engagement

3. **Sentiment analysis**
   - Positive/negative/neutral ratio
   - Common themes in feedback
   - Advocates vs. critics

### Weekly Review (April 10)

**Actions:**
1. **Aggregate performance data**
   - Total reach (impressions + engaged users)
   - Conversion funnel (views → clicks → signups)
   - Channel comparison (Reddit vs. HN vs. LinkedIn)

2. **Identify patterns**
   - Which posts performed best?
   - Which subreddits drove most engagement?
   - What feedback themes emerged?

3. **Document learnings**
   - What worked (replicate)
   - What failed (avoid)
   - What to test in Week 3

## Crisis Protocol

### If Reddit post gets downvoted heavily

**Immediate actions (within 30 minutes):**
1. Stop posting in that subreddit
2. Analyze downvote reasons (review comments)
3. Adjust approach for next post
4. Don't delete post (looks suspicious)

### If HN launch gets negative traction

**Immediate actions (within 1 hour):**
1. Respond honestly to criticism
2. Don't engage in arguments
3. Take feedback seriously
4. Document lessons for next launch

### If conversion rate is low (<2%)

**Investigation steps:**
1. Verify UTM tracking is working
2. Check demo URL stability
3. Review landing page performance
4. Survey community about friction points
