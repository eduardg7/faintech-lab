## 1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how Faintech Lab ("we," "us," "our") uses cookies and similar technologies on our website and application. By using Faintech Lab, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy.

## 2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us:
- Remember your preferences
- Understand how you use our service
- Improve your experience
- Provide essential functionality

## 3. Types of Cookies We Use

### 3.1 Essential Cookies (Always Active)

These cookies are necessary for the Service to function properly. You cannot opt out of these cookies.

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration |
|-------------|---------|----------|
| `session_id` | Maintains your logged-in session | Session |
| `csrf_token` | Prevents cross-site request forgery | Session |
| `auth_token` | Authenticates your account | 30 days |
| `preferences` | Stores UI preferences | 1 year |

### 3.2 Functional Cookies

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization.

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration |
|-------------|---------|----------|
| `theme` | Remembers your theme preference | 1 year |
| `language` | Remembers your language selection | 1 year |
| `sidebar_state` | Remembers sidebar position | Session |

### 3.3 Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how users interact with our Service.

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration |
|-------------|---------|----------|
| `_ga` | Google Analytics - distinguishes users | 2 years |
| `_ga_*` | Google Analytics - maintains session state | 2 years |
| `usage_stats` | Aggregated feature usage | 30 days |

### 3.4 Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that set their own cookies:

| Provider | Purpose | Privacy Policy |
|----------|---------|----------------|
| Google Analytics | Website analytics | [Google Privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) |
| Intercom | Customer support chat | [Intercom Privacy](https://www.intercom.com/legal/privacy) |

## 4. Cookie Consent

### 4.1 First Visit
When you first visit Faintech Lab, you will see a cookie consent banner allowing you to:
- Accept all cookies
- Accept only essential cookies
- Customize cookie preferences

### 4.2 Managing Preferences
You can change your cookie preferences at any time by:
- Clicking "Cookie Settings" in the footer
- Accessing your account settings
- Clearing your browser cookies

## 5. Legal Basis for Cookie Use (GDPR)

Under GDPR, we rely on the following legal bases:

| Cookie Type | Legal Basis |
|-------------|-------------|
| Essential | Contractual necessity (Article 6(1)(b)) |
| Functional | Consent (Article 6(1)(a)) |
| Analytics | Legitimate interest (Article 6(1)(f)) or Consent |
| Third-Party | As specified by third-party providers |

## 6. Cookie Duration

### 6.1 Session Cookies
These cookies are deleted when you close your browser:
- `session_id`
- `csrf_token`
- `sidebar_state`

### 6.2 Persistent Cookies
These cookies remain on your device until they expire or you delete them:
- `auth_token` (30 days)
- `preferences` (1 year)
- `_ga` cookies (2 years)

## 7. How to Manage Cookies

### 7.1 Browser Settings
Most browsers allow you to:
- View cookies stored on your device
- Delete individual or all cookies
- Block cookies from specific sites
- Block all cookies

### 7.2 Browser-Specific Instructions

**Chrome:** Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data

**Firefox:** Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data

**Safari:** Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data

**Edge:** Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and site data

### 7.3 Impact of Blocking Cookies
Blocking cookies may affect your experience:
- You may need to log in more frequently
- Preferences may not be saved
- Some features may not work correctly

## 8. Do Not Track

We currently do not respond to "Do Not Track" signals from browsers. However, you can opt out of analytics cookies through our cookie consent mechanism.
