# Faintech Lab - Data Subject Rights (GDPR)

**Effective Date:** March 24, 2026
**Version:** Beta 1.0

---

## 1. Introduction

This document explains your rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) regarding your personal data processed by Faintech Lab. As a data controller, Faintech Solutions SRL is committed to respecting and facilitating these rights.

## 2. Your Rights Overview

Under GDPR, you have the following rights:

| Right | Article | Summary |
|-------|---------|---------|
| Right to Information | Art. 13 & 14 | Know how your data is processed |
| Right of Access | Art. 15 | Obtain a copy of your data |
| Right to Rectification | Art. 16 | Correct inaccurate data |
| Right to Erasure | Art. 17 | Request deletion of your data |
| Right to Restrict Processing | Art. 18 | Limit how we use your data |
| Right to Data Portability | Art. 20 | Receive data in a portable format |
| Right to Object | Art. 21 | Object to certain processing |
| Rights Related to Automated Decisions | Art. 22 | Human review of automated decisions |
| Right to Withdraw Consent | Art. 7(3) | Withdraw consent at any time |
| Right to Lodge a Complaint | Art. 77 | Complain to a supervisory authority |

## 3. How to Exercise Your Rights

### 3.1 Contact Information

**Data Protection Contact:**
- Email: privacy@faintechlab.com
- Subject Line: "Data Subject Rights Request"
- Response Time: Within 30 days (extendable to 90 days for complex requests)

### 3.2 Verification

To protect your data, we may ask you to:

During beta, you can exercise some rights directly in the app:

## 4. Right of Access (Article 15)

### 4.1 What You Can Request

You have the right to obtain:
- Confirmation that your data is being processed
- A copy of your personal data
- Information about processing purposes, categories, recipients, retention periods, and your rights

### 4.2 Response Format

We will provide your data in:
- **Standard format:** JSON or PDF
- **Alternative formats:** Upon request (CSV, plain text)

### 4.3 Response Time

- **Beta period:** Within 30 days
- **Complex requests:** Up to 90 days (with notification)

## 5. Right to Rectification (Article 16)

### 5.1 What You Can Correct

You can request correction of:
- Inaccurate personal data
- Incomplete data (by providing a supplementary statement)

### 5.2 How to Request

1. Email privacy@faintechlab.com with "Rectification Request" in subject
2. Specify the data to be corrected
3. Provide the accurate information
4. Include any supporting evidence (optional)

### 5.3 Response Time

We will respond within 30 days, either:
- Confirming the correction, or
- Explaining why correction is not possible

## 6. Right to Erasure (Article 17) - "Right to Be Forgotten"

### 6.1 When You Can Request Erasure

You can request erasure when:
- Data is no longer necessary for original purpose
- You withdraw consent (and no other legal basis exists)
- You object to processing (and no overriding legitimate interests)
- Data was unlawfully processed
- Erasure is required by law
- Data relates to a child (for information society services)

### 6.2 Exceptions

We may refuse erasure when processing is necessary for:
- Exercising freedom of expression and information
- Compliance with legal obligations
- Establishment, exercise, or defense of legal claims
- Public interest (public health, archiving, scientific research)

### 6.3 Scope of Erasure

Upon erasure request, we will:
- Delete your account and associated data
- Notify third parties who have received your data (where feasible)
- Retain only data required for legal compliance (anonymized)

### 6.4 Beta-Specific Notes

During beta:
- Data backup retention: Up to 90 days post-deletion
- Anonymized usage data may be retained for product improvement
- Analytics data will be anonymized within 30 days

## 7. Right to Restrict Processing (Article 18)

### 7.1 When You Can Request Restriction

You can request restriction when:
- You contest the accuracy of data (pending verification)
- Processing is unlawful (but you prefer restriction to erasure)
- We no longer need the data (but you need it for legal claims)
- You have objected to processing (pending verification of grounds)

### 7.2 What Restriction Means

When processing is restricted, we may only:
- Store your data
- Process with your consent
- Process for legal claims
- Protect rights of others
- Process for important public interest

## 8. Right to Data Portability (Article 20)

### 8.1 What Data Is Portable

You can request your data in a portable format when:
- Processing is based on consent or contract, AND
- Processing is carried out by automated means

### 8.2 Available Formats

We will provide your data in:
- **Primary:** JSON (structured, machine-readable)
- **Secondary:** CSV (spreadsheet-compatible)
- **Upon request:** XML, plain text

### 8.3 Direct Transmission

Where technically feasible, we can transmit your data directly to another controller at your request.

## 9. Right to Object (Article 21)

### 9.1 Grounds for Objection

You can object to processing based on:
- Legitimate interests (including profiling)
- Direct marketing (absolute right)
- Research/statistics (public interest)

### 9.2 Marketing Objections

You have an absolute right to object to:
- Direct marketing communications
- Profiling for marketing purposes

To opt out of marketing: Email privacy@faintechlab.com with "Stop Marketing"

### 9.3 Legitimate Interest Objections

When you object to processing based on legitimate interests, we must:
- Stop processing unless we demonstrate compelling legitimate grounds
- Document our reasoning if we continue processing

## 10. Rights Related to Automated Decisions (Article 22)

### 10.1 Automated Decision-Making in Faintech Lab

Faintech Lab uses automated processing for:
- AI-powered memory categorization
- Semantic search ranking
- Agent behavior recommendations

### 10.2 Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Not be subject to decisions based solely on automated processing that produce legal effects or similarly affect you
- Obtain human intervention
- Express your point of view
- Contest the decision

### 10.3 How to Request Human Review

1. Email privacy@faintechlab.com with "Automated Decision Review"
2. Specify the decision you want reviewed
3. We will respond within 14 days with human review results

## 11. Right to Withdraw Consent (Article 7(3))

### 11.1 When Consent Applies

Consent is our legal basis for:
- Voice/audio recording for agent training
- Location data collection (if applicable)
- Marketing communications
- Non-essential cookies

### 11.2 How to Withdraw Consent

- **In-app:** Settings → Privacy → Manage Consent
- **Email:** privacy@faintechlab.com
- **Marketing:** Unsubscribe link in emails

### 11.3 Effect of Withdrawal

- Withdrawal does not affect lawfulness of prior processing
- We will stop the processing that relied on consent
- You may lose access to features that require consent

## 12. Right to Lodge a Complaint (Article 77)

### 12.1 Supervisory Authority

If you believe your rights have been violated, you can complain to:

- Email: anspdcP@dataprotection.ro

### 12.2 Before Complaining

We encourage you to contact us first at privacy@faintechlab.com. We will attempt to resolve your concerns directly.

## 13. Children's Data

### 13.2 Parental Rights

Parents or guardians may exercise data subject rights on behalf of their children by contacting privacy@faintechlab.com.
