## Overview

Faintech Lab ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your personal data and privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use Faintech Lab during our beta testing phase.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Account Information
- **Email address or internal user ID** - for authentication and access control

- **Usage data** - session information, interaction history, and feature usage patterns

### 1.2 Content and Memories

### 1.3 Technical Data
- **Device information** - browser type, operating system, screen resolution
- **Log data** - IP address, access times, request details

### 1.4 Behavioral Data (Beta-Specific)
During beta testing, we analyze interaction patterns to improve our AI systems. This includes:
- **Semantic search patterns** - how you search and retrieve memories
- **Agent interaction behavior** - which agents you use most and how you engage with them
- **Response effectiveness** - which AI responses are helpful vs. unhelpful

**Note:** This behavioral analysis is used to improve our product and is not used for targeted advertising or sold to third parties.

## 5. Your GDPR Rights

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the following rights:

### 5.1 Right to Access (Article 15)
You can request a copy of all personal data we hold about you, including:
- What data we have
- How we use it
- Who we share it with
- How long we keep it

**How to exercise:** Use the data export API (beta implementation) or contact privacy@faintech.ro

### 5.2 Right to Rectification (Article 16)
You can request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.

**How to exercise:** Contact privacy@faintech.ro with details of the correction needed

### 5.3 Right to Erasure (Article 17)
You can request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten"). We will delete:
- All account information
- All stored memories and text content
- All behavioral patterns associated with your account
- All audio recordings

**Exceptions:** We may retain some data if required by law or for legitimate business purposes (e.g., fraud prevention, dispute resolution).

**How to exercise:** Use the account deletion feature (beta implementation) or contact privacy@faintech.ro

### 5.4 Right to Restrict Processing (Article 18)
You can request that we restrict processing of your data while we verify its accuracy or legal basis.

**How to exercise:** Contact privacy@faintech.ro

### 5.5 Right to Data Portability (Article 20)
You can request your data in a structured, commonly used format (e.g., JSON, CSV).

**How to exercise:** Use the data export API (beta implementation) or contact privacy@faintech.ro

### 5.6 Right to Object (Article 21)
You can object to processing based on "legitimate interest" (e.g., behavioral analysis for product improvement).

**How to exercise:** Contact privacy@faintech.ro with your objection. We will stop processing unless we can demonstrate compelling legitimate grounds.

### 5.7 Right to Withdraw Consent (Article 7)
If you have consented to processing (e.g., voice input), you can withdraw consent at any time:

- **Voice input:** Disable in account settings
- **Other consents:** Contact privacy@faintech.ro

**Effect:** Withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal.

### 5.8 Right to Lodge a Complaint (Article 77)
You have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority (e.g., [National Authority for the Supervision of Personal Data Processing in Romania](https://www.anspdcp.ro/)).

**Primary contact:** ANSPDCP (Romanian DPA)
**Secondary contact:** Your local national data protection authority

## Appendix A: Technical Details (For Interested Beta Users)

### A.1 Vector Embeddings
Faintech Lab uses vector embeddings (AI-generated numerical representations) to enable semantic search. These embeddings:
- Capture the semantic meaning of text, not the exact words
- Are mathematically derived and cannot be directly read by humans
- Enable "smart" search that understands context and intent

**Privacy Implication:** Embeddings are derived from your text content and may be considered personal data. We treat them as such under GDPR and implement appropriate safeguards.

### A.2 Behavioral Analysis
We analyze interaction patterns to improve our AI systems:

| Analysis | What We Track | How We Use It |
|----------|---------------|----------------|
| Search relevance | Query-response pairs | Improve search ranking |
| Agent effectiveness | Which agents solve which problems | Route queries to best agents |
| Response quality | User feedback (thumbs up/down) | Fine-tune AI models |
| Usage patterns | Feature adoption, time-of-day usage | Optimize performance |

**Privacy Implication:** This analysis uses aggregated, anonymized data wherever possible. Individual behavioral data is retained for 12 months maximum.

### A.3 Voice Processing (Beta-Specific)
Voice input uses automated speech recognition (ASR):

1. **Recording:** Audio is captured from your device
2. **Processing:** ASR converts audio to text on our servers
3. **Analysis:** Voice patterns may be analyzed to improve recognition accuracy
4. **Storage:** Original audio is retained for 90 days for training, then deleted
5. **Use:** Text transcription is used as your input (like typing)

**Privacy Implication:** Voice biometrics are considered "special category data" under GDPR. We require explicit consent and provide opt-out at any time.
