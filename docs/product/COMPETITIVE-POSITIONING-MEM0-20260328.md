## Opportunity Statement

Mem0 Pro announced 5x price increase ($49 → $249/month). This creates a 48-72h window to capture disaffected users seeking alternatives before they commit to annual contracts or migrate elsewhere.

---

## Hypothesis

**If** we position AMC as the developer-friendly, EU-compliant alternative to Mem0 at 80% lower cost, **then** we will capture 5-10% of Mem0's disaffected user base (estimated 500-1000 users considering alternatives), **because** price-sensitive developers will actively seek alternatives within 72h of price increase announcement.

---

## AMC Competitive Advantages

### Price Advantage (80% cheaper)
- **AMC:** $49/month (maintained pricing)
- **Mem0 Pro:** $249/month (5x increase)
- **Savings:** $200/month ($2,400/year)

### Compliance Advantage
- **AMC:** EU data residency, GDPR compliant, Romanian hosting
- **Mem0:** US-based, GDPR compliance unclear for EU users
- **Target:** EU-based developers with data residency requirements

### Developer Focus
- **AMC:** Built for autonomous AI agents, multi-agent coordination
- **Mem0:** General-purpose memory, less specialized for agent workflows
- **Target:** Developers building AI agent systems
