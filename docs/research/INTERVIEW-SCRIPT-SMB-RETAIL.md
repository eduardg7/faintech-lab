# SMB Retail Customer Interview Script

**Purpose:** Validate SMB Retail hypothesis about workflow coordination and knowledge loss pain points
**Target:** SMB Retail owners/managers (shops, boutiques, specialty stores)
**Value Range:** €500-€2,000/month for AI-powered workflow orchestrator
**Segment:** SMB Retail (1-50 employees, €50K-€5M revenue)

---

## Interview Setup

**Duration:** 10-15 minutes
**Format:** Phone or video call
**Interviewer:** Sales (Growth Lead)
**Goal:** Confirm/deny hypothesis: SMB retail businesses will pay €500-€2,000/month for AI workflow orchestrator

---

## Interview Script

### Opening (1-2 minutes)

"Hello [Name], thank you for taking the time to speak with me today.

I'm [Name] from Faintech Solutions, and we're building a tool for retail business owners who are struggling with coordinating operations between multiple systems - things like inventory, marketing, and customer management.

We're looking to understand if what we're building would actually help businesses like yours. Your feedback will directly shape our product roadmap.

I have 5 short questions - should take about 10-12 minutes. Is that okay with you?"

---

### Question 1: Tool Stack Fragmentation (2-3 minutes)

"Let me start with your daily operations.

**How many different tools or apps do you use to run your business?** Things like POS system, inventory management, email marketing, accounting, calendars, etc.

*Wait for response*

**How would you describe the relationship between these tools?** Do they talk to each other automatically, or do you find yourself manually moving data between them?

*Wait for response*

**How much time per week would you estimate you spend on manual data entry or fixing sync errors between systems?**

*Wait for response*

**Follow-up if pain expressed:**
"That sounds frustrating. Can you give me a specific example of when this caused a problem - maybe missed a promotion, double-ordered inventory, or lost customer info?"

*Listen for concrete story*

---

### Question 2: Staff Knowledge Loss (2-3 minutes)

"Now, a different topic - your team.

**How do you handle information when staff members leave or go on vacation?** Do you have a system for documenting their knowledge, or does it get lost?

*Wait for response*

**Can you tell me about a time when this caused a real problem?** Maybe someone forgot a supplier agreement, didn't know about a holiday promotion, or repeated a mistake a previous employee had already solved?

*Listen for concrete example with business impact*

**If you could solve this problem completely, how much would it be worth to you per month?** Just ballpark.

*Wait for response - if they resist pricing, ask range*

---

### Question 3: Manual Coordination (2 minutes)

"Let me ask about planning and promotions.

**When you plan a seasonal promotion or sale, what's your process?** How do you coordinate between inventory levels, marketing materials, staff schedules, and pricing?

*Wait for response*

**How long does that typically take from idea to launch?**

*Wait for response*

**What's the biggest friction point in that process?**

*Listen for pain - manual steps, delays, miscommunication*

---

### Question 4: Multi-Team Collaboration (2 minutes)

"Now, thinking about your team structure.

**How does your owner/manager coordinate with your marketing lead and store manager?** Do they use a system, or is it mostly email, WhatsApp, and meetings?

*Wait for response*

**Have you ever had a situation where they weren't aligned?** Maybe marketing promoted out-of-stock items, or the owner didn't know about a store issue until it was too late?

*Listen for concrete misalignment story*

---

### Question 5: Pricing and Willingness (2 minutes)

"Let me summarize what I'm hearing. *[Briefly recap 1-2 specific pain points they mentioned]*

If we could build a tool that:
- [Recap pain 1 from their response]
- [Recap pain 2 from their response]
- [Recap pain 3 from their response]

**What would that be worth to you per month?** I know it's hard to say exactly, but just ballpark - €500, €1,000, €1,500?

*Wait for response*

**If it worked well and saved you [specific time/money example from interview], what would you be willing to pay for it?**

*Wait for response*

**Would you be willing to try a beta if we offered it at no cost for 2 months?**

*Capture yes/no*

---

## Hypothesis Scoring

### Pain Point Confirmation (PASS/FAIL)
**Criteria:** Interviewee confirms ≥2 of 4 pain points:
- [ ] Tool stack fragmentation (5-8+ apps, manual data entry)
- [ ] Staff knowledge loss (turnover = lost information)
- [ ] Manual coordination overhead (promotion planning takes weeks)
- [ ] Limited multi-team collaboration (misalignment between owner/manager/marketing)

**Score:** ___/4 confirmed
**Threshold:** ≥2/4 = PASS

### Willingness-to-Pay (PASS/FAIL)
**Criteria:** Interviewee expresses willingness to pay ≥€500/month for solution
- [ ] Stated willingness to pay €500+/month
- [ ] Stated specific value range including €500+

**Score:** ___/1 confirmed
**Threshold:** ≥1 = PASS

### Overall Hypothesis Validation
- [ ] PASS (≥2 pain points confirmed + willingness to pay ≥€500)
- [ ] FAIL (<2 pain points OR unwilling to pay)
