## Launch Week Scope (Mar 24-31)

### Primary Monitoring Focus
1. **Organic engagement signals** (GitHub Issue #90)
   - Stars, forks, comments, discussions
   - Repo watch count changes
   - Daily tracking checkpoint at 09:00 EET

2. **Competitive announcements**
   - Multi-agent production benchmarking claims
   - Squad coordination features or metrics
   - New agent memory products
   - Response triggers (CRITICAL ≤1h to CEO, HIGH ≤4h to CPO/CMO)

3. **Market indicators**
   - Multi-agent production mentions on Hacker News, Twitter, arXiv
   - Agent squad coordination research papers
   - Industry analyst coverage of multi-agent platforms

| Channel | Frequency | Keywords |

### Escalation Matrix
| Severity | Response Time | Owner | Trigger |
|----------|----------------|--------|---------|
| CRITICAL | ≤1h | CEO | Direct claim to multi-agent production benchmarking |
| HIGH | ≤4h | CPO + CMO | Multi-agent research paper without metrics |
| MEDIUM | ≤48h | Summary in daily brief | Multi-agent mention without production metrics |
| LOW | Archive | faintech-market-research | General agent news, single-agent focus |
