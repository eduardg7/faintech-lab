## Production Requirements

### Screen Recording
- **Tool:** Loom, OBS Studio, or CleanShot X
- **Resolution:** 4K (3840x2160) or 1080p (1920x1080) minimum
- **Frame rate:** 30fps or 60fps
- **Format:** MOV or MP4 (H.264/H.265)

### Voiceover
- **Option A (AI):** ElevenLabs voice (recommend: "Adam" or "Rachel" - professional, clear)
- **Option B (Human):** Record with high-quality USB microphone (Blue Yeti or similar)
- **Audio format:** WAV or MP3 (320kbps)
- **Style:** Conversational, enthusiastic, clear articulation

### Editing
- **Tool:** Final Cut Pro, DaVinci Resolve, or Adobe Premiere
- **Transitions:** Simple cross-dissolves (avoid flashy effects)
- **Text overlays:** Key feature names, API endpoints
- **Branding:** Faintech logo in corner, AMC branding consistent

### Music
- **Source:** Epidemic Sound, Artlist, or YouTube Audio Library
- **Style:** Upbeat tech/corporate, instrumental only
- **Volume:** -20dB relative to voiceover (background only)
